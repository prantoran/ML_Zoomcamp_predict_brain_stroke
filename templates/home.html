Brain Stroke Prognosis

Project info (github repo)

Model trained with 3984 observations using Brain Stroke Dataset from Kaggle.

Are you worried about your or family member's health or chances of stroke and take precautionary steps?
With this model, you can provide the mentioned features such as age, bmi, etc,
and get an approximate probability of brain stroke.
This project was made for learning and fun purposes and is not a production service
